Platform Capabilities

Intelligent Energy Management Platform

From real-time metering to carbon accounting — a complete energy intelligence stack for manufacturers.

Real-Time Energy Monitoring

Sub-meter level electricity, gas, steam, and compressed air monitoring with 15-second resolution — giving you granular visibility into every machine's energy draw.

Peak Demand Management

AI predicts demand spikes up to 30 minutes ahead and automatically reschedules non-critical loads to avoid expensive peak demand charges from utilities.

Carbon Footprint Tracking

Automatic Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions calculation aligned with GHG Protocol — with BEE/PAT compliance reporting and carbon reduction scenario modelling.

Energy Benchmarking

Normalize energy consumption by production output (kWh per unit) and benchmark against industry standards, shifts, and lines to identify your biggest inefficiencies.

Anomaly & Waste Detection

ML models flag energy waste events — compressed air leaks, idle equipment left on, inefficient motor loads — automatically triggering maintenance or operations alerts.

Energy Cost Forecasting

Forecast next month's energy bill by plant, shift, and product line — enabling accurate production budgeting and tariff optimization strategies.
