Platform Capabilities

AI Quality Control Across Every Stage

From incoming material inspection to final product release — intelligent quality monitoring at every checkpoint.

Statistical Process Control

Real-time SPC charts (X-bar, R, CUSUM, EWMA) with automatic control limit calculation, violation detection, and process capability (Cpk) scoring.

Computer Vision Inspection

Deep learning models trained on your defect images — detecting surface defects, dimensional non-conformances, and assembly errors at line speed with >98% accuracy.

Defect Pattern Recognition

AI clusters defect types by root cause categories — machine, material, method, measurement, environment — surfacing systemic issues before they escalate.

Compliance & Traceability

Full product traceability from raw material batch to finished good — with automated compliance reports for ISO, IATF 16949, FDA 21 CFR, and BIS standards.

Supplier Quality Monitoring

Incoming material quality dashboards with supplier scorecards, rejection trend analytics, PPAP tracking, and automated supplier notification workflows.

Quality Prediction Engine

Predicts final product quality from in-process measurements, enabling early intervention — stopping defective batches before completion instead of catching them at final inspection.
