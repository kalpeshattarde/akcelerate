Platform Capabilities

How Our AI Monitors Your Machines

A complete predictive maintenance stack — from raw sensor data to actionable maintenance work orders.

Real-Time Sensor Monitoring

Ingest data from vibration, temperature, pressure, current, and acoustic sensors at up to 10,000 readings/second with <100ms latency.

ML Anomaly Detection

LSTM neural networks learn each machine's unique normal behaviour and flag deviations with 85% prediction accuracy before failures occur.

Automated Work Orders

When a failure probability threshold is crossed, the system auto-generates CMMS work orders and notifies maintenance teams via SMS, email, or app.

Equipment Health Scoring

Live health scores (0–100) for every machine, displayed on intuitive dashboards with trend history, remaining useful life estimates, and drill-down analytics.

ERP / CMMS Integration

Native connectors for SAP PM, IBM Maximo, Oracle EAM, and Infor. REST APIs available for any custom CMMS or ERP system.

Root Cause Analytics

Explainable AI (XAI) surfaces the top contributing sensor signals and historical patterns behind every alert — giving your engineers full context, not black-box outputs.
