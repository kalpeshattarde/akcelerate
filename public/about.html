Common Questions

Everything you need to know before taking the next step with AKcelerate.

Manufacturing analytics eliminates guesswork by providing real-time visibility into every production variable. By identifying bottlenecks, waste patterns, and underperforming equipment automatically, your teams can act on facts rather than intuition — driving continuous improvement across throughput, quality, and costs.
Our platform collects vibration, temperature, pressure, and electrical data from equipment sensors. AI models trained on historical failure patterns analyze this data in real-time, detecting subtle anomalies that precede failures — often days or weeks in advance. You receive actionable alerts with recommended maintenance actions before costly breakdowns occur.
AKcelerate integrates with virtually any enterprise system: ERP (SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics), MES (GE, Rockwell, Siemens), SCADA and historian systems, IoT/IIoT platforms (AWS IoT, Azure IoT Hub, PTC ThingWorx), CRM platforms, and custom data sources via REST APIs or direct database connections.
Industry benchmarks show manufacturers achieve 315% ROI over 3 years. Common outcomes include 35% reduction in unplanned downtime, 15-20% productivity improvement, 23% reduction in operational costs, and 28% improvement in inventory turnover. Your specific ROI depends on current operational maturity and scope of deployment.
A typical initial deployment takes 8–16 weeks from project kickoff to first dashboards going live. We use a phased approach — delivering quick wins in the first 4 weeks while building the full platform in parallel. Enterprise-wide rollouts across multiple plants typically take 6–12 months.
