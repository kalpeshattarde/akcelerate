Background

The Story Behind AKcelerate

Kalpesh Attarde founded AKcelerate with a clear mission: to bring enterprise-grade AI analytics within reach of every Indian manufacturer, regardless of size. Having worked extensively with data science across multiple industries, he saw firsthand how massive the gap was between what technology could do for factories and what most plants were actually using.

Indian manufacturing — from automotive tier suppliers to FMCG processors to pharma manufacturers — generates enormous volumes of production data every day. But without the right analytics infrastructure, that data sits unused while downtime, defects, and energy waste quietly erode margins.

AKcelerate was built to solve this problem. Not with off-the-shelf dashboards from global vendors designed for Fortune 500 companies, but with a platform engineered specifically for the realities of Indian manufacturing — the data sources, the infrastructure, the budgets, and the operational rhythms.
